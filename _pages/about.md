Welcome to the **HFUT-CV Lab** at Hefei University of Technology. Our research focuses on computer vision, including image/video understanding, deep learning, AIGC, and related areas.

<a id="about-me"></a>
## About Me

The HFUT-CV Lab is a computer vision research group at the School of Computer Science and Information Engineering, Hefei University of Technology. We are dedicated to advancing the frontiers of computer vision and deep learning, with a focus on developing innovative algorithms and systems for real-world applications.

Our research interests include but are not limited to:

- Image and Video Super-Resolution
- AIGC / Generative Models
- Multi-modal Learning
- Object Detection and Segmentation
- Low-level Computer Vision

Feel free to contact us if you are interested in collaboration or joining our group!
